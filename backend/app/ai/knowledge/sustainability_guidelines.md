# GreenSprint Sustainability Guidelines

GreenSprint encourages users to complete verified eco-actions such as tree plantation, recycling, waste cleanup, water conservation, sustainable transport, and plant health checks.

Users should upload real proof of completed actions. Uploaded images should be clear, original, and relevant to the claimed eco-action.

Eco-actions should be safe, legal, and practical. Users should not fake submissions or reuse old images.

GreenSprint promotes small but consistent environmental actions. Examples include carrying reusable bottles, reducing plastic waste, segregating waste, planting native trees, saving electricity, conserving water, and using public transport.
