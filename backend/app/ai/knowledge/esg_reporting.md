# ESG Reporting

ESG stands for Environmental, Social, and Governance.

In GreenSprint, environmental metrics include CO2e saved, trees planted, water saved, waste reduced, and biodiversity score.

Social metrics include participants, campaign members, community posts, and user engagement.

Governance metrics include verification coverage, AI review status, admin moderation, audit logs, and report publication.

AI can explain ESG reports in simple language for organizations and admins.
